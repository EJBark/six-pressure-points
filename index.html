The Six Pressure Points Self-Assessment

This assessment diagnoses how well your organisation is set up to do what it's supposed to do — across six dimensions that determine whether strategy becomes execution.

  • 1DirectionDoes your organisation know where it's going?
  • 2DesignIs your structure built for the work you actually do?
  • 3GovernanceWho decides what, and does it actually work?
  • 4CapabilityDo you have the right people doing the right work at the right level?
  • 5DeliveryIs work actually getting done, and do you know why when it isn't?
  • 6ImprovementDoes your organisation learn, or just repeat?

The assessment takes approximately 20–30 minutes. Answer based on evidence — what you know to be true, not what you'd like to be true. Your report is generated automatically when you finish.

Before We Begin

What brought you here?

One question to frame everything that follows.

Growth, restructure, new leadership, strategy shift, performance concerns — whatever brought you here.

1 of 6 · Direction

Does your organisation know where it's going?

These questions assess whether strategic direction is clear, current, and understood across your organisation.

If none, say so. If yes, briefly describe.

2 of 6 · Design

Is your structure built for the work you actually do?

These questions assess whether your organisational design supports or hinders the work.

3 of 6 · Governance

Who decides what — and does it actually work?

These questions assess whether decision-making in your organisation is clear, timely, and appropriately distributed.

e.g. "3 meetings — one operational update, one project review, one client discussion"

If none, say so. If yes, describe briefly.

4 of 6 · Capability

Do you have the right people doing the right work at the right level?

These questions assess whether your organisation has the capability it needs — and whether it's deployed effectively.

Approximate annual percentage, or a description if you don't track it formally.

Describe what was outsourced and the reason (gap in skills, capacity, speed needed, etc.).

Be honest — are they doing work at their level, or below it?

5 of 6 · Delivery

Is work actually getting done — and do you know why when it isn't?

These questions assess your organisation's capacity to execute reliably, visibly, and to standard.

Be specific where you can. What stalled, and what caused it?

Include project management tools, task systems, and communication tools used for work management.

6 of 6 · Improvement

Does your organisation learn — or just repeat?

These questions assess whether your organisation captures what it learns and uses it to get better over time.

Be honest — what keeps coming back despite effort to fix it?

Final Step

Ready to generate your report

Your answers have been captured across all six dimensions. When you submit, your diagnostic report will be generated automatically — this takes approximately 30 to 60 seconds.

What happens next: Your responses will be analysed across the Six Pressure Points framework. You'll receive a diagnostic report identifying where pressure is concentrated in your organisation and what that pattern suggests about your current state. Recommendations are not included in this report — that comes in the Diagnostic Session.
